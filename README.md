### 🔬 Research Focus

I study how to grow better crops using fewer resources — especially in dry areas where water, energy, and nutrients are limited.

**What I work on:**
Pulse Crop Agronomy · Weed Management in Arid Pulses · Conservation Agriculture · Water-Energy-Food-Nutrient-Carbon Nexus · Dryland Farming · Resource Use Efficiency · Climate-Smart Agriculture · Soil Health

### 🌍 What Drives Me

Farmers in dry regions face tough choices every day — how to save water, reduce costs, manage weeds, and still grow enough food. My research and tools aim to help them and the scientists who support them make better, data-driven decisions.
